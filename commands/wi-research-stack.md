# /wi-research-stack

Mode: `interactive`

Use when a technology choice needs a real comparison instead of a guess.

Invoke `wi-research-stack` and follow it exactly.

Return:

- comparison criteria
- strengths, weaknesses, and trade-offs
- saved research in `.whytcard/projects/{id}/research/` when project-scoped

Non-negotiables:

- official docs first
- data over preference
- community caveats stay clearly marked
- do not claim exhaustive understanding after partial reading
